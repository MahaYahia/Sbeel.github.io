WASTE WATER CLEANING DEVICE
'PROTOTYPE'

The Safy prototype is a simple, yet efficient, solar-powered system designed to treat agricultural wastewater. Its primary function is to remove sediments and impurities, making the water suitable for irrigation and other agricultural purposes.

Initial Filtration

The design begins with a fine mesh that removes large sediments and debris from the incoming water.

Cagulation Process

A coagulant is then mixed into the water to bind smaller particles, allowing them to settle at the bottom of the tank. This process ensures that fine particles are aggregated and easily removableâ€‹.

Sediment Removal

After coagulation, the remaining sediments are collected at the bottom of the tank and removed to prevent recontamination.

Advanced Filtration

The final step involves passing the water through a large filter, designed to remove excess salts and chlorine. This filter ensures that the water is purified and made suitable for agricultural use, particularly for plants that are sensitive to chlorine or saline content.
